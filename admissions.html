FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free demo class available?

+
Yes! Every student is invited to attend one free demo class before they commit to any batch. This lets you experience our teaching style and get to know the faculty before deciding.

What is the batch size?

+
We cap batches at 20 students for Class 9–10 programs and 15 students for JEE and NEET programs. This ensures every student gets personal attention during class.

Are study materials included in the fee?

+
Yes, all printed study materials, notes, and practice papers are included in the program fee. There are no hidden costs for materials.

Do you offer scholarships?

+
Yes. Students who scored 90% or above in their previous board exam are eligible for a merit scholarship of up to 20% fee waiver. Sibling discounts and need-based scholarships are also available — contact us to know more.

Can I pay in monthly instalments?

+
Absolutely. We offer easy monthly EMI options for all programs. There is no interest charged on the instalment plan. Details are provided during the counselling session.
